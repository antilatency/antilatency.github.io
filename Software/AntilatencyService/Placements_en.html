Placements

Use the AntilatencyService's tab to set the Placement of the Alt for any VR gadget. You can choose whether one of the preloaded presets, or create your own configuration.
This tab is similar to the Environments. Here you can set the position of the Alt, the default Placement and share your project with other users.
